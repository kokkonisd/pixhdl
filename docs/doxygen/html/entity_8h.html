◆ direction

enum direction

Enumeration of the possible directions of a signal.

The possible directions of a signal (port) are IN, OUT and INOUT. However, an entity can also have GENERIC inputs; thinking of GENERIC as an additional direction helps generalize the code (adding a Signal to an Entity, printing an Entity etc).
